Who We Are

Born in Kampala,
Built with Passion

Grace Collections is Kampala's premier destination for luxury footwear, bags, and accessories. Nestled in the vibrant neighbourhood of Mutungo, we curate only the finest pieces for women who demand both style and quality.

Every item in our store is handpicked — never mass-ordered — ensuring our clients always find something rare, beautiful, and uniquely theirs.

Meet the Founder

The Woman
Behind Grace

Grace is a Kampala-born fashion entrepreneur with an eye for beauty that cannot be taught. After years of sourcing the finest pieces from across the continent and beyond, she opened Grace Collections in Mutungo to bring world-class style home to Uganda.

Her curation philosophy is simple: if it doesn't make you feel extraordinary, it doesn't belong in the store.
